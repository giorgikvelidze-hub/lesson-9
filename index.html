ABOUT US

The Schools need is a new age 'tool'

Team SchoolerPro was born out of the desperation, doubt and anxiety that afflicted Schools when COVID-19 became a reality. But it is designed to outlast not only under the pandemic but also re-imagine pedagogy for the New Generation of Students. Indian Schools, though may fare a lot of criticism for the means of imparting education, they still remain as powerhouse of educating so many students.

At SchoolerPro, we believe that what the Schools need is a new age 'tool' that helps in managing content and making it seamlessly available for their students to consume. SchoolerPro is imagined as a platform that listens - Teacher, Students, Administrator, Third Party content creator with the sole aim of re-inventing a child's education. All this without turning the pockets of parents in any way as much go much.

MISSION AND VISION

Creating tomorrow's solutions for today.

At SchoolerPro, we believe in creating tomorrow's solutions for today. Education in India has always been a fast-paced industry where new numbers are added against each other by institutions and upcoming EdTech Startups alike. We live to Differ. We are a platform that lets Teachers explore their passion, create content for students in an easy, real-world and scalable way. Maths has already done a great job of already giving kids a very Plastic brain that just needs to be nourished. We just have to keep feeding those guns and kids can imagine a world even better and smarter than us.

Our logo emphasizes our approach - 'Emphasize Application'. Our logo is devolved from a rather simple equation invented by someone from India. Yes! Pythagoras is as well known in India, thanks to his a²+b²= c'2 formula. In a poetically obnoxious way, this marks the single greatest rule named formula by many students in India. But Pythagoras is more than a formula, it's a rather simple answer to a complex poetic question as we learned them. Our logo is based on a Pythagoras Cup. When filled under a certain level, the glass will hold the liquid but if you fill it at a complex social problem of greed. That is high art. Which is what we aspire to be the field of education.
